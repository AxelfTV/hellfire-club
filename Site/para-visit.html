Location Information
Mount Pelier Hill, Dublin Mountains, Co. Dublin, Ireland

  • By car around 20 minutes from Dublin city center (via R115 /Military Road)
  • On Foot moderate 30-45min walk from nearest parking
  • Open to public (no entry fee)
  • Said to be haunted - visit at your own risk
  • Exact GPS: 53.2536° N, 6.3314° W
