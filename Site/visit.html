Location Information

Mount Pelier Hill, Dublin Mountains, Co. Dublin, Ireland
  • By car around 20 minutes from Dublin city center (via R115 /Military Road)
  • On Foot moderate 30-45min walk from nearest parking
  • Open to public (no entry fee)
  • Said to be haunted - visit at your own risk
  • Exact GPS: 53.2536° N, 6.3314° W

Hiking Trails

  • Nature Trail – 2.8 km
    A shorter route exploring the forested area, suitable for casual walkers.
  • Riverside Walk – 3.2 km
    A scenic trail following a river, offering a peaceful nature walk.
  • Forest Loop – 4.9 km
    A longer loop through the woods, providing a mix of forest tracks and views.
  • Montpelier Loop – 4.2 km
    The main trail leading to the Hellfire Club ruins, offering panoramic views of Dublin.
